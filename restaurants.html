Top Restaurants in Bali

Bali is known for its vibrant food scene, with a wide variety of restaurants serving traditional Indonesian cuisine as well as international flavors. Here are some of the best restaurants in Bali:

  1. Locavore - Located in Ubud, Locavore is a fine dining restaurant that focuses on using locally sourced ingredients to create innovative dishes. The restaurant offers a set menu that changes frequently to reflect the availability of seasonal ingredients.
  2. Sarong - Sarong is a modern Asian restaurant located in Seminyak, Bali. The restaurant offers a mix of Indonesian and Southeast Asian cuisine, with dishes like crispy pork belly and green papaya salad. Sarong also has an extensive cocktail list and a wine cellar with over 300 bottles.
  3. Mamasan - Mamasan is a stylish restaurant in Seminyak that serves contemporary Asian cuisine. The menu features dishes from China, Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand, as well as some fusion dishes. The restaurant also has a bar with a wide selection of cocktails and spirits.
  4. Merah Putih - Located in Seminyak, Merah Putih is a stunning restaurant that showcases the best of Indonesian cuisine. The restaurant's menu features traditional dishes from across the archipelago, such as beef rendang and nasi goreng. Merah Putih's interior is also worth noting, with soaring bamboo pillars and a dramatic ceiling that's meant to evoke the waves of the nearby ocean.
  5. Kilo Bali - Kilo Bali is a contemporary restaurant in Seminyak that serves international cuisine with an Asian twist. The menu includes dishes like tuna tartare and slow-cooked lamb shoulder, as well as some vegetarian options. The restaurant also has a bar with a great selection of cocktails and mocktails.

    6. These are just a few of the best restaurants in Bali. With its diverse culinary scene, Bali offers something for everyone, whether you're looking for traditional Indonesian cuisine or international flavors.
